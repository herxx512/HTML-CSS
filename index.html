The Former "Big Three" of Anime

What does "Big Three" mean?

The "Big Three" was a term used to describe the biggest and the most influential animes of the era. This term was created during the mid-2000's, which 3 Shonen Jump anime were considered to be in the golden age of anime. Each anime was picked and considered to be the best by fans all around the world.

Who are the Big Three?

During the mid-2000s, Naruto, Bleach, and One Piece were bestowed the title "The Big Three". All three animes centered around their own protaganist where each of them goes through a bumpy journey. The genre of these 3 animes are adventure, fantasy fiction, action fiction, and comedy.

Naruto is a Japanese manga series that is written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. The story of Naruto, is about a young ninja named Naruto Uzumaki, whom all the villager feared and hated. He bears the Nine-Tail Fox, a powerful and uncontrollable beast whom destroyed the Village the night Naruto was borned. His ultimate dream is to become the Hokage, the leader of the Hidden Leaf Village who is recognise and acknowledge by the whole village.

Bleach is written and illustrated by Tite Kubo. The story follows a teenager Ichigo Kurosaki, who decided to obtain the power of a Soul Reaper to help protect his love ones. After the obtaining the power of a Soul Reaper, he came across new problems, made new friends, and unfold mysteries of his past and the Soul Society. Ichigo goes on a rocky journey to learn about the Soul Society.

One piece is written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It is about Monkey D. Luffy, a young boy with the power of rubber whom one day wishes to be the King of the Pirates. As a little kid, he idolize a powerful pirate Red-Haired Shanks. Luffy eventually sets off on a journey, becoming a pirate and gathering his own crew to find the One Piece.

The debate about a new "Big Three"

Nowadays, anime have branched into different popular genres. Each year, the number of anime fans are growing rapidly worldwide. The same is happening to the number of anime produced each season. Everyone has their own taste when it comes to their favorite top three anime. Thus, the title "The Big Three" lost its true meaning and representation. Today, many anime fans after the 2010's thinks that "The Big Three" is just another way of saying "What are the top three anime of this season or in the past few years?". This then created multiple lists of unofficial "The Big Three".
Purpose
The purpose of this article is to show the history of what the title "The Big Three" truly means and how he came to be known as something different.
